### Page 2: Gelcoat Surface Preparation and Application Process

**Health & Safety Recommendations:**
* 3M E-A-R™ Ultrafit™ Earplugs, 32 dB, Corded, 50 Pairs/Box, UF-01-000
* 3M Maintenance Free Half Mask, FFA2P3 R D Filters, 4255+
* 3M SecureFit 400 Safety Glasses, Black/Green frame, Anti-Scratch/Anti-Fog, Clear Lens, SF401AS/AF-EU
*Note: Not a complete list. Select protective eyewear, appropriate gloves, hearing protection, respirator and protective clothing based on your job and exposure assessment.*

**Process Steps:**

1.  **Cleaning:**
    * Clean the new external composite surface with approved cleaner to remove all mold release materials.
    * Products: 3M™ Perfect-It™ Boat Wash, 3M™ Perfect-It™ High Performance Cloth.

2.  **Sanding:**
    * Sand the old or new gelcoat to remove surface imperfections using a DA sander with 3M™ Hookit™ 775L Cubitron™ II abrasive discs, grade 80+.
    * Apply dry guide coat to surface and re-sand to ensure an even texture.
    * Clean the repair area with approved cleaner or blow off using pressured air.
    * Products: 3M™ Hookit™ 775L Cubitron™ II Abrasive Discs, 3M™ Sander, 3M™ Hookit™ Soft Interface Pad, 3M™ Dry Guide Coat.

3.  **Filler application:**
    * If needed, mix 3M™ Marine Blister Repair Filler and apply. Fill 85% of the cavity.
    * After 30 minutes, feather the repair with 3M™ Hookit™ 775L Cubitron™ II abrasive disc, grade 80+.
    * Apply dry guide coat and re-sand. Clean the area.
    * Mix 3M™ Marine Premium Filler and apply, starting with a tight coat.
    * After filler has cured, sand with 3M™ Hookit™ 775L Cubitron™ II grade 120+ abrasive discs.
    * Products: 3M™ Marine Blister Repair Filler, 3M™ Marine Premium Filler, 3M™ Hookit™ 775L Cubitron™ II Abrasive Discs, 3M™ Dry Guide Coat.

4.  **Final filler repair:**
    * Finish by sanding the repair area with 3M™ Hookit™ 775L Cubitron™ II grade 220+.
    * Products: 3M™ Sander, 3M™ Hookit™ 775L Cubitron™ II Abrasive Discs.

5.  **Masking:**
    * Ensure the surface is completely dry.
    * Mask areas with 3M™ Precision Masking Tape first, then back it up with 3M™ Performance Industrial Masking Tape 301E or 3M Scotch® Performance Masking Tape 244.
    * Mask the entire surface with 3M™ Purple Premium Plus Masking Film.
    * Products: 3M™ Tapes, 3M™ Purple Premium Plus Masking Film.

6.  **Gelcoat application:**
    * Mix gelcoat in a 3M™ PPS™ Series 2.0 disposable spray cup (remember to remove filter in the lid).
    * Adjust 3M™ Performance Spray Gun settings.
    * Hold spray gun 15cm-20cm from the surface and apply gelcoat.
    * Products: 3M™ PPS™ Series 2.0 Spray Cup System, 3M™ Performance Spray Gun, 3M™ Performance Gravity HVLP Atomising Head.

7.  **Gelcoat finishing:**
    * For the gelcoat finishing process, please refer to the following page.

### Page 3 & 4: Gelcoat Finishing Process (5 or 6 Step Options)

**6-Step Process:**

1.  **Cleaning:** Clean the new external composite surface with approved cleaner.
2.  **Sanding refinement:** If needed, sand with 3M™ Hookit™ 775L Cubitron™ II 320+ and then 400+ grade abrasives with a Soft Interface Pad. If the surface is in good condition, start with P800 grade 3M™ Hookit™ Flexible Abrasive Foam Discs.
3.  **Sand scratch refinement:** Refine the P800 grade sand scratch using P1200 grade 3M™ Hookit™ Flexible Abrasive Foam Discs.
4.  **Compounding:** Compound the surface with 3M™ Perfect-It™ Gelcoat Heavy Cutting Compound and a 3M™ Perfect-It™ Low Linting Wool Compounding Pad. Use a Rotary Polishing machine at 1400-1800 rpm.
5.  **Polishing:** Polish with 3M™ Perfect-It™ Gelcoat Compound + Polish and a wool compounding pad at 1400-1800 rpm.
6.  **Protection:** Apply 3M™ Perfect-It™ Boat Wax to a 10cm x 10cm area and wipe clean before it dries.

**5-Step Process:**

1.  **Cleaning:** (Same as 6-step process)
2.  **Sanding:** (Same as 6-step process)
3.  **Sand scratch refinement:** (Same as 6-step process)
4.  **Compounding & Polishing:** Compound the surface with 3M™ Perfect-It™ Gelcoat Compound + Polish and a 3M™ Perfect-It™ Low Linting Wool Compounding Pad. Use a Rotary Polishing machine at 1400-1800 rpm.
5.  **Protection:** (Same as 6-step process)

### Page 5: Marine Paint Surface Preparation and Application Process

1.  **Cleaning:** Clean the surface with approved cleaner.
2.  **Sanding:** Using a DA sander with 3M™ Hookit™ 775L Cubitron™ II 80+ grade abrasive discs, sand the surface. Clean the surface after.
3.  **Filler application:** Apply 3M™ Marine Premium Filler. After it sets, apply dry guide coat and sand with 120+ grade, then finish sanding with 220+ grade. Clean the surface.
4.  **Masking:** Ensure the surface is dry. Apply masking tapes and film as described in the gelcoat process.
5.  **Primer Application:** Mix primer in a 3M™ PPS™ Series 2.0 cup and apply with a 3M™ Performance Spray Gun held 15cm-20cm from the surface.
6.  **Primer Sanding:** Apply dry guide coat and sand the primer with 320+ grade abrasive discs, then repeat with 400+ grade. Clean the surface.
7.  **Marine paint Application:** Mix paint in a 3M™ PPS™ Series 2.0 cup and apply with a 3M™ Performance Spray Gun.
8.  **Marine Paint finishing:** Refer to the following page for the finishing process.

### Page 6: Marine Paint and Clearcoat Finishing Process

1.  **Cleaning:** Clean the surface with approved cleaner.
2.  **Sanding (Dry or Wet option):**
    * **Dry:** Sand with 3M™ Hookit™ Purple Finishing Film Disc 260L+ P1200/1500 grade with a DA sander and soft interface pad.
    * **Wet:** Sand with 3M™ Trizact™ Hookit™ Clear Coat Sanding Disc grade 1500 with a DA sander and soft interface pad, using a light mist of water.
3.  **Scratch refinement (Dry or Wet option):**
    * **Dry:** Refine P1200 scratches with 3M™ Hookit™ Flexible Abrasive Foam Disc P2000 grade.
    * **Wet:** Refine P1500 scratches with 3M™ Trizact™ Hookit™ Foam Abrasive Disc 443SA grade 3000, using a light mist of water. Clean the surface before compounding.
4.  **Compounding:** Compound with 3M™ Perfect-It™ Fast Cut Plus Extreme and a 3M™ wool or green foam compounding pad. Use a rotary polisher at 1400-1800 rpm.
5.  **Polishing:** Polish the surface with 3M™ Perfect-It™ Ultrafina SE Polish and a 3M™ wool or blue foam polishing pad at 1400-1800 rpm.
6.  **Protection:** Apply 3M™ Perfect-It™ Boat Wax to a 10cm x 10cm area and wipe clean before it dries.

### Page 7: Marine Bottom Paint (Antifouling) Preparation and Application Process

1.  **Cleaning:** Clean the surface with an approved cleaner.
2.  **Sanding:** Sand the bottom surface using a dual-action sander with 3M™ Hookit™ 775L Cubitron™ II 220+ grade abrasive. Clean the area.
3.  **Filler application:** Mix and apply 3M™ Marine Premium Filler. After it cures, sand with 120+ grade abrasive, then finish with 220+ grade.
4.  **Masking:** Ensure the surface is dry. Apply masking tapes and film as described in previous processes.
5.  **Bottom paint application:** Mix bottom paint in a 3M™ PPS™ Series 2.0 cup (remove lid filter). Use a 3M™ Performance Spray Gun, holding it 15cm-20cm from the surface to apply the paint.
