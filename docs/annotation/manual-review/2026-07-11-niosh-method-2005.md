## Eligibility decision

strong confirmatory source candidate. Its current gold is not evidence eligible. It
omits mandatory safety precautions, a dilution-factor action, and the concentration
formula; its span excludes the precautions and ends inside an unrelated reference; and
its linear relation chain erases conditional, calibration, quality-control, and per-lot
