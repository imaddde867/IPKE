## Third-Party Material Screen

Federal works are not copyrightable under 17 USC 105, but they may embed
third-party images, tables, vendor marks, or vendor photos. Before
publishing extracted text, redact or replace any non-federal material
and keep the original URL in the manifest.

## Excluded Sources

- `MyFixit` and `iFixit` repair content are excluded from this corpus
  and from training, evaluation, and annotation transfer without
  explicit written permission.

## How to Update This File

When adding a new source, append a row to the table above and add an
attribution block to `ATTRIBUTION.md`. Open a PR so the change is
reviewable.
