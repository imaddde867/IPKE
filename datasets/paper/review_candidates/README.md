# Agent-prepared review candidates

Files here reduce primary-review transcription. They incorporate source audits, exact
item anchors, and high-confidence repairs, but remain `unreviewed` candidates. They are
not human evidence and are never consumed by confirmatory evaluation.

A primary reviewer must read the complete frozen source span, decide every proposed
item, inspect negative regions, resolve the accompanying decision packet, and save the
separately attributable output under `datasets/paper/primary_pass/` with a matching
evidence package.
